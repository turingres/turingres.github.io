What's mowvli™?

mowvli™ is a first-of-its-kind mobility operating system.


What's a mobility operating system?

Just like you don't get a computer simply by throwing in some processors, memory chips and hard disks without an operating system to tie them all together, you don't get a smart mobility system simply by having a multimodal fleet. A mobility operating system optimizes, operates and manages the fleet in real-time - it ties all the parts together into a seamless and unified multimodal system of on-demand mobility.


That's cool!

We think alike!


But what if the fleet is autonomous?

Smart cars don't make smart mobility by themselves! A mobility operating system is all the more necessary.


So how does mowvli make real-time decisions?

Great question! There are some really powerful algorithms and models that power its brain. But honestly, mowvli has mastered the "jungle book" well - it draws much of its power by synthesizing Artificial Intelligence (AI) with Operations Research (OR).
