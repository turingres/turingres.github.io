Who We Are

We are a "Science-powered Technology" company.

Mobility is a fundamental social, economic, environmental and technological challenge. We are tackling this challenge head-on through science.

Our innovations span and synthesize scientific disciplines such as Artificial Intelligence, Operations Research, and Algorithms. And we are taking our innovations out of the lab to disrupt mobility.

We also publish some of our research to advance scientific knowledge. Check out our sample publications below.

When Hashing Met Matching: Efficient spatio-temporal search for ridesharing

This work introduced the powerful AI technique of Locality Senitive Hashing (widely used in several engineering disciplines) to mobility research. Efficient high-dimensional search technology developed in this paper aids real-time urban-scale mobility optimization.
