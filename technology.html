Our vision

We are a "science-first" company.

Mobility is a fundamental societal challenge. A true disruption for this challenge must be powered by deep-tech built on scientific innovations.

Our innovations span and synthesize disciplines of Artificial Intelligence, Operations Research, Algorithms and more. And we take these innovations out of the lab to real life.

While these innovations build the secret sauce for our techniology, we also contribute to advance scientific knowledge in related fields. Check out some of our publications below.

Edge Weighted Online Windowed Matching

This work proposed a novel online algorithmic model to study real-time decision making in matching markets such as mobility platforms. It developed several algorithmic techniques in the new model and has generated a lot of research interest in the area.

When Hashing Met Matching: Efficient spatio-temporal search for ridesharing

This work introduced the AI technique of Locality Senitive Hashing (widely used in fields such as image search, information retrieval, machine learning) to mobility literature.
